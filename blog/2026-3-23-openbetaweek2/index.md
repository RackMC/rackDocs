Welcome to week 2 of the RackMC Open Beta. Over the past week, we have had **30+ unique players** join and over **200 hours** of total playtime on the network! We just wanted to say thank you for dealing with all the bugs and being patient with us. Now that a big chunk of issues have been fixed, it should be mostly smooth sailing from here.

Here is a list of all the bug fixes and changes that have been made since our last post on Friday. If you find any more issues, make sure to open a ticket in our [discord](https://discord.rackmc.net). 

## Major Changes
### Lands
- Nation creation cost has increased from $100 to $1000
- Chunk claiming cost remains $100, but each additional chunk will only cost $10 more instead of $25
- Added a 5th vote site
- Updated the vote crate reward pool
- You can now show off items by typing `[i]` in chat
- Land upkeep has increased from $5 per chunk to $10 per chunk
- Jobs payouts have been reduced by half to promote more mining and trading between players

## Known Issues
### Lands
- `/rtp` does not work (use `/wild` for now)
- The MCMMO Mining skill levels up too quickly
- The smelting part of the crafter job does not pay out for an auto smelter
- The fortune enchanment on a pickaxe conflicts with MCMMO double drops

## Bug Fixes
### Lands
- Monsters that wander into claimed land with spawning disabled will despawn
- `/wild` has changed from 500:2500 blocks to 1000:5000 blocks
- Vote message formatting has been adjusted
### Creative
- You can now spawn up to 20 mobs in your plot
