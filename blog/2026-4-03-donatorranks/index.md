## Why Time Based Ranks?
We have made it our primary focus to not only provide no competitive advantage from buying a rank, but we also wanted to make ranks cheaper and more easily accessible to all. That being said, ranks are how we are able to raise funds to improve the server and overall experience. 

We felt that this was the best way to accomplish our goal, and with how our perks work, we feel as though this will still allow you to feel as though its worthwhile.

## What Do The Ranks Offer?
Our primary perk and cosmetic is the network wide tags that you can unlock. While you unlock the tags by purchasing a rank (or directly purchasing tags from the store page), you don't lose your tags when your rank expires. 

When you purchase a rank, you will receive tag "credits" in game. The number of credits you receive depends on which rank you purchase and for how long. With the credits, you can purchase any tag available on the tag shop. 

### Iron
Iron grants you the following:
- [Iron] prefix in tab and chat
- 1 tag credit per 30 days
- Improved Daily Rewards in Lobby
- 10% Off Mystery Box Crafting in Lobby

### Gold

## Disclaimer
All donator ranks are a work in progress, so they are subject to change between now and the launch of the server. Features such as how credits work and the tag shop are not currently implemented and may change before RackMC's launch. 

We do not currently have any donator tags, therefore the purchase of Netherite will not grant you any tags until we release some. 

We do not have all planned donator features implemented as of now, so Netherite may be lacking features that were listed above until RackMC's launch.

**We can guarantee that the purchase of the Netherite Rank will grant you all donator perks forever upon the launch of RackMC.**
