Month 3 of the RackMC Open Beta is here! There have been ups and a *lot* of downs. The server may have been down for over a week last month, but we are back and still moving forward! The server has been stable for the past 5 days, so we feel confident to reschedule the Factions Playtest to **start May 9th at 6pm EST!** It will run for 30 days, ending on June 8th at 6pm EST. For more info, visit the [previous post here](https://rackmc.net/blog/factionsplaytest)

Here is a list of what has happened over the past month, as well as any planned changes for this month:

## Upcoming Changes
### Factions
- **Factions Season 0 will launch May 9th @ 6pm EST!**

## Bug Fixes
### Network
- The server works again!
